About me

I work at the interface of nutritional biochemistry and spectroscopy-enabled artificial intelligence to enable rapid, non-destructive screening of nutritional traits in crop germplasm.

My research focuses on NIRS/FTIR-coupled prediction modeling, germplasm screening, and mainstreaming underutilized crops for sustainable food and nutrition security.
